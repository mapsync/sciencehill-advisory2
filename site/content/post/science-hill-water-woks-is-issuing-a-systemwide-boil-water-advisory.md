NOTICE ⚠️ The City of Somerset is issuing a systemwide boil water advisory that begins Saturday, Jan. 13, and lasts until further notice. All water districts in Pulaski County are included.

The advisory is mandated by the Kentucky Division of Water due to equipment installation at the Somerset Water Plant High Service Pumping Station. 

Beginning Saturday, residents are urged to boil water used for drinking and cooking for three minutes before consuming.
