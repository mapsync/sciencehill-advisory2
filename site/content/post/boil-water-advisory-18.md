Science Hill Water Works has issued a Boil Water Advisory from the intersection of Standford St and Hwy 635 west to the intersection of Hwy 1676. This will include Apache Dr. and Scenic Dr. and all side roads





Please bring all water used for consumption to a rolling boil for 3 minutes prior to use.
