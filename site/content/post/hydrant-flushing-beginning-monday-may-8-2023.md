Notice of Hydrant Flushing Beginning Monday May 8, 2023 Science Hill Water Works will be flushing hydrants in the Science Hill Water Works System. The flushing will continue until Friday June 2, 2023. Hydrant flushing may cause discolored water, please check the clarity of the water prior to washing clothes. The flushing is required to enhance the quality of the water as well as to ensure the operational conditions of the fire hydrants.
